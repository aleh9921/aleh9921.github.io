What is the Little Ghost?

The Little Ghost cafe is a pop-up coffee shop that not only focuses on making great coffee but on creating great experiences, and a pleasant atmosphere.
During our appearances, let your imagination free with our beautiful scented crayons and doodle away at the Little Ghost.

Cafe favorites

Cafe Viennois

Fancier than the average latte, the Cafe Viennois is a creamier option, made from espresso, hot milk, and whipped cream.

Ghastly Merengue

The pale yet impressive ghastly merengues are made of Brown sugar with white chocolate mousse and coffee syrup.

Hibiscus Strawberry

This beautiful pink iced drink brings the refreshing fruity flavour of hibiscus tea. All brought together by coconut milk and sliced strawberries to make the flavours really sing.
